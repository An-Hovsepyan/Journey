Terms

Tour Cost is listed in per person for groups*, Double/Twin accommodations in hotels.

The price includes:

  1. Quality hotel accommodations
  2. Comfortable vehicles (van, minibus, bus)
  3. Meals (depending on a tour)
  4. Entrance fees to cultural and historical monuments (depending on a tour)
  5. Travel Insurance
  6. Professional services of a multi-lingual Tour Guide who accompanies the group.

Single supplement (Single accommodation in hotels) will be in up to 25 percent (for non-special-offer tours the surcharge can be higher).

Up to 20 percent discount for children under 12.

Transportation

Comfortable Vans, Buses or Minibuses, air-conditioned and furnished with comfortable seats.

Other Conditions

Pre-payment/Deposit is 30-50 percent of the tour cost payable up to 30 days or earlier before the tour departure date. Customers agree that specific times and the sequence of visiting cities and attractions in the program may vary depending on the circumstances of the actual tour. However, none of the cities and attractions will be missed (except in cases where their attendance will be impossible for reasons beyond the tour operator's control). Depending on a situation of a specific tour hotel accommodations can be changed to an equal or higher level.

There should be at least 10 persons in a group (for non-special-offer tours).

Please call us or send us an email to inquire about how many tourists have booked for the day of your preferred departure.

If on the departure day there are less than the specified minimum number of tourists in the group, tourists will be offered an alternative of departing with any of the next scheduled tour days.

Or, to guarantee the departure, surcharges will be applied to the base price.
